# Smart Grievance System (SGS)

### AI-Powered Chief Minister Grievance Dashboard

An intelligent grievance redressal and monitoring platform designed to help the Chief Minister's Office monitor, prioritize, track, and resolve citizen complaints efficiently while reducing false closures, corruption, and administrative delays.

---

## Problem Statement

Chief Ministers receive thousands of grievances across departments every day. Existing complaint systems often suffer from:

* Complaint duplication
* Delayed resolution
* False complaint closures
* Lack of accountability
* Poor visibility of officer workload
* Absence of real-time escalation mechanisms
* Difficulty identifying critical life-threatening issues

The Smart Grievance System addresses these challenges through AI-assisted complaint management, automated workflow assignment, and executive-level monitoring dashboards.

## Unique Innovations

### Critical Life-Threatening Alert System

Automatically identifies complaints related to:

* Road accidents
* Open electrical hazards
* Waterlogging emergencies
* Fire incidents
* Public safety threats

These complaints are immediately escalated to relevant authorities and highlighted on the CM dashboard.

### Officer Bandwidth Monitoring

Tracks:

* Active complaints
* Resolution speed
* Pending workload
* SLA violations

Helping prevent artificial delays and unfair workload distribution.

### Location-Aware CM Dashboard

When the CM visits an area, nearby unresolved complaints can be displayed automatically, enabling data-driven field inspections.

### Future Social Media Integration

Complaints originating from social media platforms can be linked and tracked within the same workflow.
